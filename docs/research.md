# 研究基礎

社群核實奠基於對「錯誤認知」、「社會規範」與「平台動態」的同儕審查研究。以下是促成並支持本設計的關鍵發現。

## 核心發現

### 認知落差(The Perception Gap)

- **Lee, Neumann, Zaki & Hancock (2025).** "Americans overestimate how many social media users post harmful content." *PNAS Nexus*, 4(12). ── 3.1% 的使用者發布有毒內容;美國人估計卻是 43%(高估 13 倍)。修正此一高估改善了正面情緒、降低對道德衰退的感知。[doi.org/10.1093/pnasnexus/pgaf310](https://doi.org/10.1093/pnasnexus/pgaf310)

- **Ahler & Sood (2018).** "The Parties in Our Heads: Misperceptions about Party Composition and Their Consequences." *Journal of Politics*, 80(3). ── 美國人對黨派刻板印象群體成員的高估達 342%。民主黨人高估有錢的共和黨人比例;共和黨人高估無神論的民主黨人比例。[doi.org/10.1086/697253](https://doi.org/10.1086/697253)

- **Moore-Berg, Ankori-Karlinsky, Hameiri & Bruneau (2020).** "Exaggerated meta-perceptions predict intergroup hostility between American political partisans." *PNAS*, 117(26). ── 黨派支持者對另一邊敵意的高估約為兩倍。認知越不準確,去人性化越強。[doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2001263117](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2001263117)

- **Sparkman, Geiger & Weber (2022).** "Americans experience a false social reality by underestimating popular climate policy support by nearly half." *Nature Communications*, 13, 4779. ── 80% 美國人支持再生能源建設,人們估計卻是 43%。[doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-32412-y](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-32412-y)

- **Yudkin, Hawkins & Dixon (2019).** "The Perception Gap." More in Common. ── 政治參與度最高的美國人,認知落差比參與度低者高出近 3 倍。媒體消費與政治參與會惡化準確度。[perceptiongap.us](https://perceptiongap.us/)

### 修正是有效的

- **Mernyk, Pink, Druckman & Willer (2022).** "Correcting Inaccurate Metaperceptions Reduces Americans' Support for Partisan Violence." *PNAS*, 119(16). ── 對黨派敵意不準確信念的單一修正,降低了對黨派暴力的支持,效果持續約 26 天。n=4,741。[doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2116851119](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2116851119)

### 沉默螺旋

- **Noelle-Neumann (1974).** "The Spiral of Silence: A Theory of Public Opinion." *Journal of Communication*, 24(2). ── 當人們相信自己持有少數立場時,會自我審查。這形成回饋迴圈,讓感知到的多數越來越大聲、實際的多數越來越沉默。[doi.org/10.1111/j.1460-2466.1974.tb00367.x](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1460-2466.1974.tb00367.x)

- **Hampton, Rainie, Lu, Shin & Purcell (2014).** "Social Media and the 'Spiral of Silence'." Pew Research Center. ── 社群媒體放大了螺旋:感知到 Facebook 網絡與自己意見不合的人,在線上與線下都比較不願發聲。[pewresearch.org](https://www.pewresearch.org/internet/2014/08/26/social-media-and-the-spiral-of-silence/)

- **Oz, Shahin & Greeves (2024).** "Platform affordances and spiral of silence: How perceived differences between Facebook and Twitter influence opinion expression online." *Technology in Society*, 76. ── 當代實證確認沉默螺旋動態在社群媒體上運作。對社會孤立的恐懼壓抑意見表達,而平台特定的可供性(網絡關聯、匿名性、社會臨場感)會調節此效應。[doi.org/10.1016/j.techsoc.2023.102431](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.techsoc.2023.102431)

### 平台上的少數壟斷

- **Grinberg, Joseph, Friedland, Swire-Thompson & Lazer (2019).** "Fake news on Twitter during the 2016 U.S. presidential election." *Science*, 363(6425). ── 在 2016 年大選期間,0.1% 的使用者分享了 80% 的假新聞。[doi.org/10.1126/science.aau2706](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.aau2706)

- **Baribi-Bartov, Munger & Pan (2024).** "Supersharers of fake news on Twitter." *Science*, 384(6700). ── 一小群帳號為大多數錯假訊息的散布負責。[doi.org/10.1126/science.adl4435](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.adl4435)

- **Bail (2021).** *Breaking the Social Media Prism.* Princeton University Press. ── 政治上最極端的使用者主導線上論述,創造出公眾意見的扭曲鏡像。[Princeton University Press](https://press.princeton.edu/books/hardcover/9780691203423/breaking-the-social-media-prism)

### 社會規範研究

- **Wojcieszak (2008).** "False Consensus Goes Online: An Analysis of the Relative Frequency Hypothesis." *Public Opinion Quarterly*, 72(4). ── 線上環境放大了錯誤共識(false consensus)效應。[doi.org/10.1093/poq/nfn056](https://doi.org/10.1093/poq/nfn056)

- **Pinker (2025).** *When Everyone Knows That Everyone Knows.* Scribner. ── 共同知識(common knowledge,即「我們知道每個人都知道」)形塑了社會規範與集體行為。讓隱藏的共識變得可見,改變了什麼事是可能的。[Simon & Schuster](https://www.simonandschuster.com/books/When-Everyone-Knows-That-Everyone-Knows/Steven-Pinker/9781668011577)
